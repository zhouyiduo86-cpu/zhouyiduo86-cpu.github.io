Lifecycle Wage Dynamics & Inequality

  • Objective: Investigated the evolution of the gender wage gap and college premium across worker lifecycles using U.S. Census microdata (IPUMS CPS).
  • Data Engineering: Built an R-based data pipeline (dplyr, tidyr) to ingest, clean, and trim a raw dataset of 200,000+ records, isolating valid full-time equivalent wages.
